# Privacy Policy

**Service:** AutoVerdict — auto-verdict.app  
**Last updated:** June 2, 2026

---

## 1. Who We Are

AutoVerdict (auto-verdict.app) is an AI-assisted screening tool that helps private buyers evaluate used-car listings. The service is operated as an independent product.

For privacy inquiries, contact: **support@auto-verdict.app**

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

When you use AutoVerdict we may collect:

| Category | Details |
|---|---|
| Account data | Email address and basic profile from Google OAuth |
| Vehicle data | Descriptions, listing URLs, and images you submit for analysis |
| Generated reports | AI analysis reports produced for your checks |
| Credit and payment records | Credit balance, purchase events (no card numbers stored by us) |
| Technical logs | Request logs, error logs, and processing metadata for debugging |

We do not collect passwords. We do not track browsing activity outside of AutoVerdict.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

- authenticate and maintain your account;
- process submitted car checks and generate AI reports;
- manage your credit balance and process payments;
- handle support requests;
- detect and prevent abuse;
- keep the service operating correctly.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 4. AI Processing Notice

Text descriptions and images you submit are sent to the **Anthropic Claude AI API** to generate your analysis report. By submitting a check you consent to this data transfer. Anthropic processes this data under their own privacy policy (anthropic.com/privacy). We do not retain raw prompt or response content by default.

---

## 5. Uploaded Files and Vehicle Information

Uploaded images and generated reports are stored in private, access-controlled object storage and are not publicly accessible. Please:

- upload only vehicle-related images;
- avoid submitting documents containing unnecessary personal data (e.g. national ID numbers, financial statements, personal contracts).

---

## 6. Payments

Credit purchases are processed by **Stripe**. Stripe receives and stores your payment card data under their own privacy policy (stripe.com/privacy). We receive only a payment confirmation event to grant credits — we do not store card numbers or CVVs.

---

## 7. Authentication

Sign-in uses **Google OAuth 2.0**. Google shares your email address and basic profile (name, profile picture) with us. We store a short-lived session token to keep you signed in. We do not receive or store your Google password.

---

## 8. Data Retention

| Data type | Retention |
|---|---|
| Account data, check history, reports | Retained while your account is active |
| Payment and audit logs | Retained as required by law or operational needs |
| Technical logs | Retained for a limited rolling window |

---

## 9. Data Deletion Requests

To request deletion of your account and associated data, email us at **support@auto-verdict.app**. We will process the request within a reasonable time, subject to any legal retention obligations.

---

## 10. Security

The service uses:

- HTTPS for all connections;
- token-based authentication;
- private object storage with access control.

As an MVP product we apply reasonable security practices, but we do not currently hold formal compliance certifications such as ISO 27001 or SOC 2.

---

**support@auto-verdict.app**
